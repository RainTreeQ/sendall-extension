Data Collection

We do not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data recorded in the extension. All operations are performed locally on your device. The extension does not use tracking cookies, analytics services, or cloud synchronization for your chat history.

Permissions

  • activeTab / tabs: Required to find and inject messages into the open AI chat windows.
  • scripting: Required to interact with the input elements on the AI chat websites.
  • storage: Required to save your extension preferences (e.g., Auto Send toggle state) locally.
  • clipboardRead / clipboardWrite: Required for advanced copy-paste interactions within the extension interface if used.
